Tank Defence

The purpose of the game is to destroy the aliens invading the city. The player controls a tank. The player can move backwards and forwards and move the tank turret up and down. The player can control the power that the canon shoots from the tank.

Tank Defence is an HTML5 Canvas game. I made most of the graphics using Photoshop, Inkscape, and Gimp. The game is written using Javascript. Tank Defence requires a keyboard. The controls are listed below. Click the tank image below to play the game.
